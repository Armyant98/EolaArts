Eola Arts Incubator

Eola Arts Incubator is a co-learning community at the intersection of business and technology. We conduct original research to examine how generative technologies impact organizations and markets.

We focus on understanding how both entrepreneurs and existing organizations can leverage technologies such as generative AI, blockchain, VR, and APIs to create and capture value for their primary stakeholders.

Research

Learn about research we are involved in

We examine this technology-organization interface as it relates to artful expression, artful organizing, and art markets. These contexts demand that people creatively transformation their cognitive, material, social resources in the face of increasingly competitive market forces. These dynamics unfold across the individual, team, organization, and levels. So art offers as a rich context as any other for examining the broader consequences of these generative technologies.

Also, these contexts are too much fun to avoid! Why do our work any other way?
